Why

TCG Matrix?

Recently, there has been a serious lack in availability of rulesets or game information for Trading Card Games (TCG) and Board Games. This is the reason TCG Matrix came into existence in the first place; To spread information about Trading Card Games and Board Games to players worldwide, in an easy access, user-friendly manner, all at the tap of a button, and all completely free of charge. This makes it much more convenient for newer players to have quick and convenient access to information they may need if they forget a rule, and for older, more experienced players to teach new players the games they already love.
